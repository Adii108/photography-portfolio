# Product Steering

## Product identity

Build a nature, trekking, and travel photography website that feels like a wild cinematic nature magazine rather than a plain portfolio.

The tone is:

- premium editorial
- adventurous
- painterly and textured
- mountain diary energy
- controlled maximalism, not clutter

## Primary user

A photographer or trekker who wants to upload one image with a caption and place, then have it appear beautifully on the public site.

## Product priorities

1. Beauty first: the site must look memorable, immersive, and polished.
2. Simplicity second: keep content fields minimal and avoid CMS bloat.
3. Fast implementation: build a strong first version without unnecessary systems.
4. Cloudflare-friendly: use Pages, Pages Functions, R2, and D1.

## Content model

A photo entry only needs:

- image
- caption, optional
- place, optional
- category, optional but useful for filters

Optional future fields:

- date
- mood
- camera
- lens
- trek name
